Spaghetti


Spaghetti comes in many variances, all with their own take on the classic italian cuisine. Meat sauces to pesto sauces, flavors that appreciate the noodle's own flavor are popular and delicious, all of which fall under the scope of spaghetti.


It's hard to say that it is not a good food. 10/10 would eat again.
