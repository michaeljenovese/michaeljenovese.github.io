Pizza


Being made since 1889

Pizza is one of the most popular American food; everyone has eaten pizza at some point in their life. Its popularity is partially due to the wide range of pizzas made, from pineapple and ham, pepperoni and bacon, to barbeque sauce and grilled chicken -- there are millions of combinations availiable.

Spaghetti


Being made since ~1300s

Spaghetti is found in different forms all over the world, from being served with a creamy basil sauce to having a tomato sauce with meatballs. It's very hard to go wrong with such an easily combinable food, and the number of flavors possible is astoudning.
